Who We Are

More than just an Italian restaurant, we're a place that aims to capture the essence of Italy's rich cultural heritage. A culture where family, love, and the joy of sharing are deeply ingrained in our restaurant.

We believe we can do things the Italian way: with passion, patience, and above all, heart. We always guarantee that every detail reflects our essence and values: authenticity, warmth, and love for our roots.

More than just a place to eat, this is a place where you can feel at home.

Questions & Answers

Everything you need to know

What are your opening hours?

+

We're open Tuesday to Sunday from 12:00 PM to 10:00 PM. We're closed on Mondays to ensure our team gets proper rest, just like in traditional Italian trattorias.

Do you offer vegetarian or vegan options?

+

Absolutely! We have several vegetarian pasta dishes and pizzas. We can also modify many of our dishes to be vegan-friendly. Just let us know your preferences when ordering.

How long does delivery take?

+

Typical delivery time is 30-45 minutes depending on your location. We prepare everything fresh to order, so your food arrives hot and delicious.

Can I make special requests for my order?

+

Of course! We're happy to accommodate dietary restrictions, allergies, and preferences. Just mention your requirements when placing your order and we'll do our best to make it perfect for you.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept cash, all major credit cards, and digital payment methods including Apple Pay and Google Pay for both takeout and delivery orders.
